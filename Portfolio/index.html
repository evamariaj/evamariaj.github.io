About

Experienced designer changing from one business to another - with an eager prospect of making a difference. 
With an international background I am used to working in multicultural teams and a commercial and creative mind is my strength. I am currently studying UX Design in Malmö in south of Sweden and will be ready for a new and exciting carreer in May 2022.
